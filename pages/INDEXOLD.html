Who am I?

Hello. My name is Luis Gonzalez and I am a second year student at the Georgia Institute of Technology.

 

Here at Georgia Tech I am currently studying to earn a BA in Computer Science. I chose to pursue an CS degree because I have always been fascinated with learning new computer languages and exploring what I can create with them. My hope is that by pursuing this degree I will one day be able to be part of a team that designs the systems that carry mankind to new frontiers within the solar system and beyond.

 

One of my favorite hobbies and research topics is coding and programming languages. I enjoy learning these new langauges by coding different applications and games that I myself would enjoy using, and as such the majority of the work found on this site are my apps and games. In my quest to learn every programming language ever created, I have learned and mostly mastered the following:

  • Haxe
  • Java
  • Lua
  • Python
  • JavaScript
  • ReactNative
  • HTML and CSS
